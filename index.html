    Melasti Beach

    Melasti Beach is a stunning destination located in Ungasan, Bali. It is famous for its dramatic limestone cliffs, pristine white sand, and crystal-clear turquoise waters. This serene beach is a hidden gem that offers breathtaking views, making it a favorite among photographers and nature lovers. Visitors can enjoy swimming in calm waters, sunbathing, or exploring the picturesque surroundings.


    Apart from its natural beauty, Melasti Beach is known for its cultural significance. The beach often hosts traditional Balinese ceremonies, where locals carry offerings down to the shore as part of religious rituals. This combination of unspoiled nature and rich culture creates a unique experience, making it a must-visit spot for anyone exploring Bali.
